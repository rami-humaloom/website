Corporate training is broken. We're fixing it.

Despite billions invested annually, employees still don't retain or apply what they learn. The reason? Training hasn't evolved for the modern workforce.

Every team is different. Every role is different. Every person learns differently. And every company has a culture that no off-the-shelf platform can teach. Most platforms call it personalization when they let you pick a course from a catalog. We don't think that counts. Real personalization means training that's built around your company's knowledge, shaped to each role, and tailored to the specific skill gaps holding each person back.

And that's exactly what we do.

Our Story

Why We Built Humaloom

We started Humaloom because we saw the same pattern everywhere: HR teams that cared deeply about their people but couldn't deliver the development those people deserved. Not because of a lack of effort or ambition, but because of a lack of resources.

Every great company has something special — a culture, a way of connecting with customers, a passion for what they do. It lives in the minds of your best people. It shows up in the way a seasoned team member tells the story behind a product, or the way they turn a first-time visitor into a loyal customer.

But here's the problem: that magic doesn't scale.

Instead, you end up relying on PDFs, outdated slide decks, and shadowing shifts — and hoping it all sticks. New hires show up full of energy but leave before they ever feel confident. The training takes too long, covers too much, and somehow still misses the point. And the customer experience you worked so hard to build? It depends entirely on who happens to be working that day, at that location.

We built Humaloom because we believe your people deserve better, and so does your business.

We believe that every company already has what it takes to train exceptional teams. The knowledge is there. The culture is there. The passion is there. What's missing is a way to take all of it and turn it into something personal — something that meets each employee where they are, and closes the gaps that stand between a new hire and a great team member.

That's what Humaloom does. We use AI to transform what you already know into training that actually works: hyper-personalized to each role, each skill gap, each person. No more one-size-fits-all programs. No more losing great people to bad onboarding. No more wondering whether your training is making a difference.

Because when your people feel confident, they connect. When they connect, your customers feel it. And when your customers feel it, your business grows.

That's not just better training. That's the future of how companies grow.
