Four Steps to Better Training

From upload to impact — here's how it works.

Step 1

You Provide Content

Upload your existing PDFs, videos, presentations, policy documents, onboarding guides — whatever you've got. Humaloom works with what you already have.

No need to create new content from scratch. Your institutional knowledge is the foundation.

Step 2

AI Analyzes & Structures

Humaloom's AI breaks down your content, identifies key concepts, and creates structured learning modules. It understands context, prerequisites, and difficulty levels.

Think of it as having a curriculum designer on autopilot — but one that knows your company.

Step 3

Employees Get Personalized Paths

Each employee gets a learning path tailored to their role, skill level, learning style, and career goals. No two paths are the same — because no two employees are the same.

Just-in-time learning means the right content arrives at the right moment.

Step 4

You Track Real Impact

Go beyond completion rates. See metrics tied to actual business outcomes: skill acquisition, performance improvement, retention rates, and time-to-competency.

Finally, data that makes leadership listen.

Three Dimensions of Personalization

Humaloom doesn't just personalize content — it personalizes the entire learning experience.

Individual-Level

Adapts to each person's learning style, preferences, and pace. Visual learner? Hands-on? Prefers reading? Humaloom adjusts accordingly.

Task-Specific

Tailored to actual job responsibilities, skill gaps, and career goals. Delivers just-in-time learning exactly when employees need it most.

Culture & Context

Aligned with your company's values and culture. Leverages your institutional knowledge. Maintains brand consistency across all training.
